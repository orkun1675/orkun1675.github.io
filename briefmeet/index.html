Description

BriefMeet helps you meet, connect, and re-engage with professional connections from networking, recruiting, and corporate events.

Easily identify each connection and never again lose track of the names and faces of those you only briefly meet. BriefMeet improves the ease and longevity of your networking experience by organizing contacts in an identifiable way, so that you can easily reconnect and build personal, sustainable relationships.

A top app used by job-seeking college students, working professionals, and recruiters, BriefMeet helps users connect seamlessly without disrupting the flow of conversation. Simply identify other users by scanning a QR code, using location-based search by name, or feeding a photograph into our facial recognition software to connect and share information instantly.

By taking advantage of information gathered from synced LinkedIn and Google accounts as well as personalized user-added tags (to record the unique interests, skills, and qualifications of each connection), our app allows users to organize and annotate their network. Utilize tags to filter and search with ease. Identify and reconnect with those met at a specific event or a subset of your network satisfying certain interests, traits, opportunities, or more. Seeking a job? Simply search your connections by company, industry, or occupation to view the profiles of those you might reach out to as well as the notes documenting your encounter.

What you can do:

- Share information with connections instantly to minimize disruption to your conversation.

- View contact information with integration to LinkedIn and Google accounts

- Add personalized tags to connections to describe their occupation, skills, interests, and other notable traits.

- Easily filter and search by event or tags to identify and view subnetworks within your professional world.

- Re-connect easily and instantly to maximize job and career opportunities.
